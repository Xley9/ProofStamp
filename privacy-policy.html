Privacy Policy

ProofStamp – Digital Evidence Protector
Last updated: January 29, 2026

Summary: ProofStamp does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. All data stays 100% on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking.

1. Introduction

ProofStamp ("the App") is a mobile and web application that allows users to capture, hash, and securely store digital evidence on their own device. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

2. Data Collection

ProofStamp does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account required — There is no registration, login, or user account.
  • No server communication — The App does not connect to any external server or cloud service. All functionality is entirely offline.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use any analytics tools, cookies, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • No data sharing — We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using the browser's IndexedDB storage:

  • Photos and files you choose to add as evidence
  • SHA-256 hashes (cryptographic fingerprints) of those files
  • Titles and descriptions you enter for each proof
  • Timestamps of when each proof was created
  • GPS coordinates (only if you explicitly grant location permission)
  • App settings (theme, language preference)

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, synced, or backed up to any server.

4. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera — To take photos of evidence directly within the App. Photos are stored locally only.
  • Location — To optionally record GPS coordinates for each proof. This is entirely optional and only activated when you tap the "Add Location" button.
  • Storage / Files — To allow you to upload files from your device as evidence.

All permissions are optional. The App functions without any of them (you can upload files instead of using the camera, and skip location).

5. Data Export

You may export your data as a JSON file or as a PDF document. These files are generated locally on your device and saved to your local storage. No data is transmitted during export.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete individual proofs or all data at any time from the Settings menu within the App. Uninstalling the App will also remove all stored data.

7. Third-Party Services

ProofStamp does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data. The only external resources loaded are:

  • Google Fonts (Inter) — For typography. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • jsPDF & qrcode.js — JavaScript libraries loaded from CDN for PDF generation and QR code creation. These run entirely in your browser and do not transmit data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction), as it does not collect data from anyone.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
