## Full Description (English)

ProofStamp is a free, privacy-first app that helps you secure digital evidence directly on your device — no account, no cloud, no data collection.

Whether it's a scratch on a rental car, damage in your apartment before moving out, a delivery that arrived broken, or a screenshot of an important conversation — ProofStamp creates a tamper-proof digital record with a unique SHA-256 cryptographic fingerprint.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Capture or upload a photo/file as evidence
2. A unique SHA-256 hash (digital fingerprint) is automatically generated
3. Timestamp, GPS location, and hash are stored securely on your device

KEY FEATURES
• SHA-256 Hashing — Every file gets a unique cryptographic fingerprint that proves it hasn't been altered
• Camera & File Upload — Take photos directly or upload existing files
• GPS Location — Optionally record the exact location where evidence was captured
• PDF Export — Generate professional PDF certificates with photo, metadata, hashes, and QR code
• Verification — Re-upload any file to verify its integrity against the stored hash
• Categories — Organize by: Apartment, Vehicle, Purchase, Communication, Contract, Workplace, Other
• Search & Filter — Quickly find any proof by title, description, or category
• Gallery & List View — Choose between visual gallery or compact list
• Dark & Light Mode — Comfortable viewing in any environment
• 5 Languages — English, German, Turkish, Spanish, French
• Offline-Ready — Works completely without internet
• Export & Import — JSON backup and restore of all your data

100% PRIVACY
• All data stays on YOUR device — nothing is uploaded anywhere
• No account or registration required
• No analytics, no tracking, no ads
• No internet connection needed
• Open source on GitHub

PERFECT FOR
• Documenting rental car condition before/after
• Recording apartment damage when moving in/out
• Keeping proof of deliveries and purchases
• Saving screenshots of important conversations
• Documenting workplace conditions
• Any situation where you need verifiable evidence

ProofStamp uses the Web Crypto API (SHA-256) to generate cryptographic hashes that mathematically prove a file has not been modified since the moment it was captured. Combined with timestamps and GPS coordinates, this creates a strong digital evidence chain.

Free. No ads. No in-app purchases. Open source.
